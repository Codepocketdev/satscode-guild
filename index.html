— What is the Guild —

Build the tools that
free the world.

SatsCode is a build-in-public community for developers who want real exposure to decentralized protocols. No gatekeeping. No corporate agenda. Just builders shipping, testing each other's work and learning on the bleeding edge of open money and open communication.

We build onramps, offramps, wallets, social tools and education platforms — anything that moves value or information without permission.
